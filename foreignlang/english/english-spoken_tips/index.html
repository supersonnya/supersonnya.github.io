英语口语技巧

Jun 19, 2020

Reading time: 14 minutes.

/t/的同化现象

  • 如果一个单词以/t/结尾，下一个单词以/p/开头。这时/t/的发音发生同化，近似于/p/。所以white piece of paper发音就变成了whi(te) piece of paper。例如：
    1. It’s a whi(te) piece of paper.
    2. There are lots of grea(t) parks in London.
    3. I love spli(t) pea soup.
    4. I ha(te) potatoes.
    5. My ma(te) picked us up from the airport.

关联/r/

  • 在war后面的单词以元音音素开头，在这种情况下，字母r发音。本土发音中使用这种方式使句子发音更流畅。例如：
    1. War and Peace.
    2. You know, I really like my mother-in-law.
    3. Have you been to the Tower of London?
    4. You’ve got something in your eye.
    5. Never again!

辅音延长/s/

  • 当一个单词以/s/结尾，下一个单词以/s/开头。 这时不拼读两个/s/的音，也不拼读一个/s/的音，而是品读比一个/s/稍长的音。例如：
    1. He looks sad.
    2. She’s silly.
    3. He’s sitting over there.
    4. Can you come this Saturday?
    5. That’s so unfair!

音节省略/t/

  • 在日常口语中, 当/t/音素出现在两个辅音之间时不发音，以使语句表达更流畅。这是因为当/t/ 在中间时，连续发三个辅音很困难。例如：
    1. I can’t do it.
    2. I can’t stand the rain.
    3. It must be time to leave
    4. Am I the first person here?
    5. I don’t want it.

插入音素/r/

  • 如果一个单词以/ɔː/音素结尾，比如law，并且下一个单词以/ə/音素开头，在中间会出现一个/r/音素来把它们连接到一起，/r/音不是特别重。比如Law-r-and order。这不会出现在所有的口音中，但可能会听到。 例如：
    1. I think law and order is important.
    2. Can you draw a circle freehand?
    3. My dog hurt its paw on some broken glass.
    4. There was a flaw in the argument.
    5. I saw a good film last night.

中性元音/ә/

  • 音素 /ә/ 是英语中最常见的发音，一般很难听到这个发音因为它不被重读。例如：
    1. It’s a piece of cake
    2. I like a cup of tea in the morning.
    3. Could you get a packet of biscuits?
    4. Can you give it to me?
    5. I had an apple for lunch today.

连接/w/

  • 当一个单词以/u:/音结尾，并且下一个单词以元音开头，此时可以在这个发音中间听到一个/w/音。但是并不是一个完整的/w/音，是因为由/u:/音过渡到元音时的口型与/w/ 音相同。例如：
    1. The shoe is on the wrong foot.
    2. When do I have to be there?
    3. I haven’t got a clue at all.
    4. That glue is really strong.
    5. I really can’t do it.

同化/n/

  • 当单词以/n/结尾并且下一个单词以/p/，/b/，/w/ 或/m/开始。结尾的/n/会变成一个/m/，以便为发下一个音做准备。Green Park会变成Greempark。例如：
    1. I own ten pairs of socks.
    2. He didn’t do it on purpose.
    3. I would love to live in Paris.
    4. Can you come on Wednesday?

音节省略/d/

  • 当/d/在两个辅音中间时发音会变的困难，一般这种情况下/d/会被省略。比如Boiled potatoes变成boilpotatoes，steamed potatoes变成steampotatoes。例如：
    1. My best friend let me borrow his car.
    2. Don’t hold back - say what you mean.
    3. His bike rolled down the hill without him.

同化/nd/

  • 对话中会听到samwich而不是sandwich。首先/d/在两个辅音之间时会消失，此时sandwich 变成 sanwich。然后，当/n/后面是/w/时，/n/会变成/m/。最后sandwich 就变成了samwich。比如：
    1. My grandparents have been married for 50 years.
    2. I got a standby ticket.
    3. The band played until midnight.
    4. I left my handbag on the train.

Have to

  • 使用have to时，have词尾的/v/变成/f/。 同时to中的元音会变成弱读元音Also/ә/。所以have to 发音变成/hæftә/。
    1. They have to be there by 10.
    2. We have to find another flat.
    3. You have to tell me the gossip.
    4. I always have to take the train.

And

  • And是一个低级连接词并且经常弱读。在元音和辅音前弱读时，词尾的/d/不发音，前面的元音发音也变成弱读元音/ә/，所以and的常见发音就变成了/әn/甚至是/n/。例如：
    1. I’m going to go and get the shopping.
    2. Apples and oranges are my favourite fruits.
    3. I’ve been thinking and thinking but I can’t decide.
    4. We went for a coffee and something to eat.

Was和were

  • 单词was发音由/w/, /ɔ:/和 / z/组成。当单词弱读的时候，元音变为弱读元音 /ә/。所以was读音变成/wәz/，were读音变成/wә/，被称作弱读式。例如：
    1. I was there when it happened.
    2. We were delighted with the results.
    3. We were having a good time until it rained.
    4. He was feeling much better last night.

Consonant-Vowel linking

  • 当一个单词以辅音结尾，下一个单词以元音开头，这两个音素被连读，中间没有停顿。an egg变成anegg。 例如：
    1. He’s in the garden.
    2. I used to believe in Father Christmas, but not any more.
    3. I had to give up jogging.
    4. He had it in his office.

同化/d/

  • 当在 /g/或/k/前时，/d/变为/g/。所以bad girl 变为bagirl。 例如：
    1. He’s a really good cook.
    2. Last night we had guests for dinner.
    3. Can you print out the hard copy?
    4. I’d love to walk down the red carpet one day.

Can

  • 当情态动词can不重读时，发音由/kæn/变为/kәn/。例如：
    1. Can you help me bring in the shopping, please?
    2. I can swim further than I can run.
    3. You can succeed if you work hard.
    4. How far can you throw a tennis ball?

爆破音

  • 爆破音有/d/``/t/ /p/``/k/ and /g/。当一个单词以爆破音结尾，下个单词以辅音开头时，会听不到爆破音的爆破。I'd like to变为Idliketuh。 这被称为无声除阻。例如：
    1. How about a quick chat?
    2. You should thank her.
    3. It was a sad time in his life.
    4. Why don’t you sit down?

The

  • The在辅音开头的单词前读/ðә/。例如：
    1. I prefer the blue one.
    2. Autumn is the best time of year.
  • The在元音开头的单词前读/ðɪː/。例如： 4. Some examples of phrases where this might happen include: 5. I always order the apple pie. 6. I prefer the orange one.
  • /ɪː/和元音之间要加/j/。例如：
    1. I always order the/j/apple pie.
    2. I prefer the/j/orange one.

Linking /j/

  • 当一个单词以/aɪ/、/ɪː/、 /eɪ/或/ɔɪ/结尾，下个单词以元音开头，添加一个/j/帮助过渡。注意是口型变化的自然过渡，并不是一个完整的/j/音。例如：
    1. I ate the whole cake.
    2. It was too high up to reach.
    3. That boy is mine.

Been

  • 当助动词been不重读时，发音为/bɪn/。弱读形式，发音更加流利自然。例如：
    1. I’ve been working here for several years.
    2. How long have you been waiting?
    3. It’s been years since I went to the cinema.
    4. What have you been up to since we last met?

同化/s/

  • 当一个/s/后跟/j/或/ʃ/时，/s/变为/ʃ/。 因为/t/在两个辅音中间时会消失，/s/后面是/t/时也可能发生这种情况。例如：
    1. Can you just shut the door please?
    2. This yacht is beautiful.
    3. Don’t worry, that’s usual.
    4. He always makes you feel good.

From

  • From一般不重读。当位于句尾时，发音仍是/frɒm/。但当在句中时，/ɒ/变为弱读元音，所以/frɒm/变为/frәm/。例如：
    1. I borrowed it from my brother.
    2. I read the book from cover to cover.
    3. The nearest post office is not far from here.
    4. Come over any time from seven o’clock.

辅音延长/t/

  • 当/t/前是元音并且下个单词也以/t/开头时，两个/t/ 一起变为一个稍强的/t/。例如：
    1. It took him twenty years to get to the top.
    2. You don’t have to cheat to pass the test.
    3. You were right to be upset.
    4. We took the overnight train to Venice.

Would you…?

  • 当一个单词以/d/结尾，下一个单词以/j/开头，两个音变成 /ʤ/。You中的/uː/可能变为弱读元音。would you变成woul/ʤ/ou或者woul/ʤә/。例如：
    1. I’m sad you decided to quit.
    2. Could you get here by midnight?
    3. Should you see him, can you give him a message?
    4. I don’t think the gold you bought was real

同化/d/和/b/

  • 如果一个单词以元音加/d/结尾并且下一个单词以/b/开头，/d/变成/b/并与下个单词的/b/合并。speed boat变为speebboat。例如：
    1. My son is a really good boy.
    2. I’m sorry if I was rude before.
    3. My car had to be towed back to the garage.
    4. You should buy him a present.

缩略have

  • have做助动词时经常缩略。当出现在辅音后时读为/әv/，I’d have been late读为I’d’ve been late，这种发音经常出现在条件句中。例如：
    1. Your parcel should’ve been delivered yesterday.
    2. I would’ve done it differently.
    3. We might’ve made a mistake.
    4. The police’ve arrived.

省略/h/

  • 当he出现在主句或从句开头时， h发音为/h/。当出现在主句或从句中并且在辅音之后时，h不发音。/hiː/变为/iː/。 其他以h开头的单词也会出现这种情况，比如him``his``her。例如：
    1. I really like his cooking.
    2. We think he’s left the country.
    3. They let him leave early.
    4. They offered her a promotion.

同化/t/

  • 当一个单词以元音加/t/结尾，且下一个单词以/k/开头，/t/变为/k/。比如/kredɪt kɑːd/变为/kredɪk kɑːd/。 例如：
    1. Don’t let it get cold!
    2. You have to do it quickly.
    3. There’s no short cut to learning a language.
    4. I didn’t like that cake.

同化/t/

  • 当一个单词以/t/结尾，且下个单词以/j/开头，比如you或yours，这两个发音会变为一个/ʧ/。meet you变为/miːʧuː/。当下个单词是you或'your时也会发生/t/在两个辅音中间消失的情况，但这种情况下/t/变为/ʧ/更常见。例如：
    1. That shirt won’t suit you.
    2. Don’t you want to come to the party?
    3. I bet your boss doesn’t know.
    4. Can I get you anything?

中性元音schwa

  • 需要尤其关注中性元音，英语发音正确的关键就包含中性元音的正确使用。
